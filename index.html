What We Do

Light cleaning. Clear sealing. Lasting protection.

We lightly clean newly finished hard surfaces to remove dust and build residue, then apply a clear penetrating sealant to tile and grout. The result is a cleaner, better-protected surface without changing the appearance of the floor.

Surface Prep

Light post-construction cleaning to remove dust and leftover build residue from finished tile surfaces.

Clear Sealing

Penetrating clear sealant applied to tile and grout to help protect against early staining and discoloration.

Move-In Ready Finish

Common Questions

Simple answers for builders and homeowners.

Does clear sealing change the look of the floor?

No. The sealant is designed to help protect tile and grout without changing the finished appearance.

When should the service be scheduled?

Typically after final clean and before homeowner move-in, so the surfaces are protected at handoff.

Who is this best suited for?

Builders, project teams, and homeowners looking to protect grout lines and preserve finish quality from day one.
